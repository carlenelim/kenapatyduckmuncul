About Us

Welcome to Ryuu'staurant, your number one restaurant for lettering. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of lettering, with a focus on services, consistency, and uniqueness. Founded in 2020 by Kyuu, Ryuu'staurant has come a long way from its beginnings in a house office. We now serve customers all over the Indonesia, and are thrilled to be a part of the creative wing of the lettering industry.
